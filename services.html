Gift deeds

A gift deed is a legal document that transfers ownership of property or assets from one person to another without any exchange of money. It's used for gifting items like real estate, vehicles, or cash. A gift deed typically involves a voluntary act of giving. A gift deeds are irrevocable once executed. Read More

License & Leave

License and leave is a temporary occupancy agreement granting one party the right to use another's property without creating a formal tenancy. It's revocable and commonly used for short-term arrangements, providing flexibility for both parties involved.
